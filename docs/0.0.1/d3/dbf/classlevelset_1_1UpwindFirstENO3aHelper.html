Computes spatial derivative.

Parameters
[out]dst_dLCell vector containing the 3 or 4 left approximations of the first derivative (each the same size as data).
[out]dst_dRCell vector containing the 3 or 4 right approximations of the first derivative (each the same size as data).
[out]dst_DDCell vector containing the divided difference tables (optional).
[in]gridGrid structure.
[in]srcSource data array.
[in]dimWhich dimension to compute derivative on.
[in]approx4Generate two copies of middle approximation using both left/right and right/left traversal of divided difference tree. The extra copy is placed in the fourth element of derivL and derivR, and is equivalent to the version in the second element of those cell vectors.
[in]stripDDStrip the divided difference tables down to their appropriate size, otherwise they will contain entries (at the D1 and D2 levels) that correspond entirely to ghost cells.
[in]loop_beginIndex of loop to begin this itteration.
[in]slice_lengthslice_length of loop to begin this itteration.
[in]num_of_slicesnumber of strides to begin this itteration.
[in]num_of_stridesnumber of DD strides to begin this itteration.
Return values
trueOperation succeeded.
falseOperation failed.
